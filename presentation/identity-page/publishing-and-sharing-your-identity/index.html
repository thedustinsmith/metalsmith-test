Publishing and Sharing Your Identity Page

Publishing Your Identity Page

Your Identity page is automatically published once you create the unique URL for your page.  Everytime you make changes, the published version is updated.  You can easily view those changes on the published version by clicking the 'Published' button in the upper right corner of the editor.

 

 

Sharing Your Identity Page

It is very easy to share your Identity Page with others.  Simply give them the URL for the Identity Page found on your dashboard.  You can put this link on your resume, business cards, or in your portfolio, so others can access the page and learn more about you.
