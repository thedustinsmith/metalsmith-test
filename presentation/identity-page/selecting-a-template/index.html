Selecting a Template

Selecting a Template

Once you've developed the URL, the next step in creating your Identity page is to select a template.  The template gives you a general layout and initial design for your identity.  From here, you can further customize the look and layout.

You can keep selecting templates until you find one you like.  Your ID page will update after each selection so you can see what it looks like.

If you ever want to 'start all over' with the look of your identity page, simply go back to the template area, pick a new one, and begin again.
