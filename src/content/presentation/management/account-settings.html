You can get to your account settings at any time through the black bar at the top of the screen.  Click your username and choose "Account Settings"

 

 

Account Settings

There are several functions found in the Account Settings area.  Here you can update your profile information, , change your password, change your license or billing information, or cancel your account.

 

Updating Your Profile

To update your username, name, or contact information, simply click in the boxes and edit the text.

 

 

 

 

Changing Your Password

To change your password, type your current password into the 'Current Password' box and type the password you wish to have in the 'New Password' and 'Confirm Password' boxes. Don't forget to click the check mark at the bottom of the screen to save any changes you make.

 

 

 

 

Passwords must be a minimum of 7 characters in length.  Any passwords containing 6 or fewer characters will not be accepted.  For security purposes, it is advisable to use at least 1 capital letter, 1 lowercase letter, and 1 number in your password.

 

Changing Your License and Billing

If you would like to change your license to add more storage space, change the billing cycle, or cancel your account, click the 'Billing' link.

 

 

You may change your license by selecting the checkmark corresponding to the extension you wish to use. You then will need to re-enter your credit card information. Once all your information is filled out, click the check mark button to confirm the changes. If you wish to cancel your account, click the red button under the payment extension options.
