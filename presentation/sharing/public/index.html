Publicly Share an Electronic Portfolio - Foliotek Presentation

Public Shares

The Public share option is located in the 'sharing' drop down.  It is labeled 'Share Publicly'.

 

 

What is a Public Share?

The Public share is used to place your portfolio on the internet for the whole world to see.  Using the public share, you will create a URL for your portofolio.

 

 

Quick Instructions:

  • type a name in the URL box
  • Click 'Validate' to ensure the name is available
  • Then, click the 'Share Portfolio' button

Now, anyone in the world can see your portfolio.  You can share this URL with someone and they can view your portfolio on the internet.  You can include it on your resume, job applications, business cards, or just share it with friends and family.

 

 

The public share also allows you to share your portfolio on Facebook, Twitter, or LinkedIn.  Once you share it publicly, you will see the Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn icons appear.  Click on the icon for the media you wish to share with, follow the prompts, and you will place a link to the portfolio in your account for that particular audience to see.

 

Feedback

You can read what comments have been made by clicking on the username in the top right corner and then clicking on 'Feedback' in the dropdown.

 

 

Ending Shares

At anytime you can easily stop sharing your portfolio publicly. Simply click the 'Manage ePortfolios' button, then 'manage share' next to your portfolio. In the box that pops up, click 'stop share'. This removes the portfolio from the Internet.  To start sharing it again, simply click the 'start sharing' link that appears next to the URL.
