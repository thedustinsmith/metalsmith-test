Merging Pages - Foliotek Presentation

Merging pages is important because this is how your portfolio takes changes you have made to a page and makes them viewable by those with whom you have shared your portfolio.  If you make changes without merging, no one can see the changes.

Merge With Shared

You can merge any changes with your shared portfolio by clicking the 'merge w/shared' link in the upper right corner of the portfolio editor, next to the 'Save' button.  If this link is orange in color, you have unmerged changes to your portfolio and need to merge them to make them live.  Otherwise, no one will be able to see your changes.

 

 

Merging on the Pages Tab

You also have the ability to merge any changes from the Pages tab.  After making changes to a page of your portfolio, you can open up the Pages tab to merge the changes.

 

 

When you open the Pages tab, take a look at the Draft pages list.  You will see '[changed]' next to any page that has unmerged changes.  This lets you know that you need to make those changes live.  When you click on the page name, the 'merge' link will appear to the right of the name in the Draft pages list.  A link called "Merge changes with shared" will also be available in the center of the Pages area.  Clicking on either of these links will update any changes on the live version of that page and make them viewable to anyone with whom you have shared your portfolio.
